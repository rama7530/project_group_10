About This Data

The data displayed in the table on this page is sourced from IMDB's Top 250 movies list. It includes information such as movie names, ratings, release dates, budgets, domestic and worldwide gross earnings, and more. Top 250 movies list

Variable Definitions

  • MoviesNames: The name of the movie.
  • Rating_out_of_10: The movie's rating on a scale of 0 to 10.
  • Count_of_Ratings: The number of ratings received by the movie.
  • Release_Date: The date when the movie was released.
  • Budget: The budget allocated for making the movie.
  • Domestic_Gross: The total gross earnings of the movie within the domestic market.
  • Domestic_Gross2: Additional information about domestic gross earnings (if applicable).
  • Domestic_Weekend_Gross: The gross earnings of the movie during its opening weekend in the domestic market.
  • Domestic_Weekend: Additional information about the domestic weekend gross (if applicable).
  • Domestic_Weekend_Gross_Date: The date when the movie's domestic weekend gross earnings were recorded.
  • Worldwide_Gross: The total gross earnings of the movie worldwide.

Note: The data provided here is for demonstration purposes only and may not reflect real-world figures accurately.
